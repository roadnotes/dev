## Safety Considerations

- **Quadra/Hillside intersection** - Six-way intersection, watch for turning vehicles from all directions
- Northbound Quadra between Bay and Hillside: narrow lanes, parked cars, impatient drivers
- **Stop 2701 (Southbound Quadra at Pembroke)** - Blind driveway immediately after stop, check mirror before proceeding
- Downtown routing through Pandora: be aware of vulnerable population, maintain compassion while ensuring safety

## Stop-Specific Information
